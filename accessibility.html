Accessibility Statement

GymTranning.com • Last Updated: November 3, 2025

At GymTranning.com, we believe that fitness should be accessible to everyone. We are committed to ensuring digital accessibility for people with disabilities. We are continually improving the user experience for everyone and applying the relevant accessibility standards.

Our Commitment

We are committed to making our website accessible to the widest possible audience, regardless of technology or ability. We are actively working to increase the accessibility and usability of our website and in doing so adhere to many of the available standards and guidelines.

This website endeavors to conform to level AA of the World Wide Web Consortium (W3C) Web Content Accessibility Guidelines 2.1. These guidelines explain how to make web content more accessible for people with disabilities. Conformance with these guidelines will help make the web more user-friendly for all people.

Conformance Status

The Web Content Accessibility Guidelines (WCAG) defines requirements for designers and developers to improve accessibility for people with disabilities. It defines three levels of conformance: Level A, Level AA, and Level AAA.

Level AA

Fully Conformant

Core features meet WCAG 2.1 AA standards

Level AAA

Partially Conformant

Some features exceed AA requirements

Continuous

Ongoing Improvement

Regular audits and updates

Accessibility Features

We have implemented the following accessibility features on our website:

Keyboard Navigation

Full website functionality accessible via keyboard without requiring mouse interaction

Screen Reader Compatibility

Compatible with screen readers including JAWS, NVDA, and VoiceOver

Text Resizing

Text can be resized up to 200% without loss of content or functionality

Color Contrast

Sufficient color contrast ratio (minimum 4.5:1) for normal text

Alternative Text

Descriptive alt text for all meaningful images and graphics

Semantic Structure

Proper heading hierarchy and semantic HTML elements

Known Limitations

Despite our best efforts to ensure accessibility of GymTranning.com, there may be some limitations. Below is a description of known limitations, and potential solutions:

Area Limitation Alternative Solution
Third-party content Some embedded videos from external sources may lack captions We provide transcript links where possible
Complex calculators Some interactive fitness calculators may be challenging for screen reader users Text-based alternatives provided
User-generated content Content submitted by users may not meet accessibility standards We moderate and provide guidance to users
Legacy content Older articles and resources may have accessibility issues Ongoing content audit and update process

Planned Improvements

We are continuously working to improve the accessibility of our website. Our upcoming improvements include:

  • Enhanced Keyboard Navigation: Improving focus indicators and keyboard shortcuts for calculators (Q4 2025)
  • Audio Descriptions: Adding audio descriptions for key workout demonstration videos (Q1 2026)
  • Sign Language Support: Including sign language interpretation for popular workout routines (Q2 2026)
  • Alternative Input Methods: Supporting voice commands and switch access (Q3 2026)
