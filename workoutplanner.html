FAQ

How does the Workout Planner work?

Choose your location, target type, goal, experience level, gender, and days per week. The planner generates a custom routine with video demonstrations.

Can I customize the generated workout?

Yes! Use the Customize button on each day to edit exercises, sets, reps, add new exercises, or remove ones you don't want.

Are the routines free?

Absolutely! All workout routines, video demos, and the planner tool are completely free.
