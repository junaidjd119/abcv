1. Understanding the Core Science

Avoid training to failure on compound barbell lifts. Lifting heavy weights recruits high-threshold motor units, stimulating neurological adaptations.

Powerbuilding splits combine heavy compound lifts (1-5 reps) with bodybuilding isolation volume sets (8-15 reps) for both strength and size. This training stimulus triggers a significant release of natural anabolic hormones.

Allow 3 to 5 minutes of rest between heavy strength sets to let ATP-CP stores fully replenish.
