1. Understanding the Core Science

SMART goals (Specific, Measurable, Achievable, Relevant, Time-bound) provide clarity. Mental preparation optimizes focus, motor unit recruitment, and pain tolerance during intense physical training.

Biological friction causes excuses. This psychological adaptation builds deep discipline and helps you resist immediate gratification.
