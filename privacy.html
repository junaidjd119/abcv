Privacy Policy

Effective date: November 3, 2025

GymTranning ("we", "us", "our") provides workout videos and fitness resources at https://gymtranning.com. This Policy explains how data may be collected and used on the site. The site displays ads served by third parties (for example, Google AdSense) and uses cookies for ad delivery and basic site functionality.

1. Cookies & advertising

We use cookies and similar technologies to deliver ads and improve user experience. Google and other third-party ad networks may set cookies when serving ads on our site. These cookies may be used for personalized advertising (showing ads based on prior visits or interests).

2. Third-party services

Some site content (for example, embedded videos) may come from third-party platforms such as YouTube or Vimeo. When you interact with embedded content or third-party ads, those third parties may collect information in accordance with their privacy policies. We do not control those third-party tracking practices.

3. What we collect

We do not maintain a user database or require account registration. However, when third parties serve ads or provide content, they may collect non-personal information such as IP address, device and browser information, and cookie identifiers. This site itself does not collect or store personal contact or payment information unless you explicitly provide it through an off-site payment flow or contact form (if added later).

4. Your choices

  • To opt out of personalized ads from Google, visit Google Ads Settings.
  • You can clear or block cookies via your browser settings; note this may reduce some functionality or prevent personalized ads.

5. Cookie banner & consent

We display a cookie consent banner to allow users to Accept or Reject non-essential cookies. If you reject cookies, personalized ads may not be shown; however, some third-party content (e.g., embedded videos) may still load and use their own cookies according to their policies.

6. Children

Our content is intended for users aged 13 and over. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If we learn of such collection, we will take steps to delete the data.

7. Changes

We may update this policy. Any material change will be posted here with a revised effective date.
