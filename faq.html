General

Is GymTranning free to use?

Yes! Our articles, calculators, and basic workout guides are completely free to use. We are supported by ad revenue.

Do I need an account?

No, you can access all our content without creating an account. We believe in open access to fitness education.

Training & Nutrition

How do I use the Calorie Calculator?

Simply enter your age, weight, height, and activity level. The calculator will estimate your TDEE (Total Daily Energy Expenditure) and suggest macros for cutting, bulking, or maintenance.

Can I request a specific workout topic?

Absolutely! Use our Contact Page to send us your suggestions.
