GymTranning didn't start in a boardroom; it started in a small. Windowless garage gym where our founders realized a frustrating truth: the fitness industry was broken. For every one piece of solid, science-based advice, there were ten fads, "quick fixes,". Dangerous "shortcuts" being marketed to unsuspecting beginners.

In 2020, we launched GymTranning with a singular vision: to build a digital sanctuary for those who want to train hard. Train smart. We stripped away the hype. Focused on the first principles of biomechanics and nutrition. What began as a simple spreadsheet of exercise tips has grown into a global resource serving thousands of lifters every month.
