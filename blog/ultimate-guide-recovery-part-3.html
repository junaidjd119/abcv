1. Understanding the Core Science

Muscle repair requires positive protein balance. Understanding this cellular repair process prevents overtraining and protects your joints from wear.

Deep sleep (N3 stage) is when Growth Hormone release peaks. Active recovery, such as light stretching or walking, increases local blood flow to tissues.

Hydrate thoroughly and consume adequate protein post-session to give your body the raw materials for repair.
