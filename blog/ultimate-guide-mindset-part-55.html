1. Understanding the Core Science

High stress elevates cortisol, a catabolic hormone that slows recovery. Mental preparation optimizes focus, motor unit recruitment. Pain tolerance during intense physical training.

Fitness is a lifelong habit. This mindset shift helps you embrace the discomfort of training as a stimulator for growth.

Establish a solid pre-workout routine to signal to your brain that it is time to focus. Train.
