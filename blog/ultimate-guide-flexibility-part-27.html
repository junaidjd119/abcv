1. Understanding the Core Science

PNF (Proprioceptive Neuromuscular Facilitation) involves contracting a muscle isometricly against resistance before stretching it. Exploiting autogenic inhibition for rapid flexibility gains. Enhancing flexibility increases the tolerance of your nervous system to stretch. Muscle fibers to lengthen safely.

Sitting causes chronic shortening of the psoas and iliacus (hip flexors). This neuromuscular adaptation helps coordinate stabilizer muscles and improves overall balance.

Sink into stretches slowly during exhalations. Hold for 20 to 30 seconds to allow the muscles to relax.
